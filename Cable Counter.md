<h1> Cable Counter </h1>


<p> Cable Counter was produced to aid when On Site installs are taking place. 
  The objective is to create a list of all the ethernet cables required without having to manually count them.</p>


  <p> How to Use: Simply count the number of devices that are included within your project.
    Simply add 2 additional for each switch. The output will include the cables that are required for a connection
    amongst all devices. </p>
